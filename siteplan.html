iMarket Site Plan

Site Name

iMarket: This name represents an innovative online marketplace that connects customers in Kenya with a variety of trusted local vendors. The "i" stands for "integrated" or "intelligent," reflecting the platform's goal of creating a seamless shopping experience.

Site Purpose

The iMarket site will serve as a central e-commerce platform where various shops, such as AutoGiant Motors for vehicles and Soko Properties for real estate, can list their products and services. Its primary purpose is to provide a comprehensive, secure, and user-friendly online shopping experience, bringing together diverse offerings under one trusted brand. The site will facilitate transactions and offer dedicated support for both vendors and customers.

Scenarios

  1. "As a customer, I want to find a car listed by AutoGiant Motors and compare it with properties from Soko Properties without leaving the iMarket platform."
  2. "I'm a new vendor and I want to understand how I can list my products on iMarket and manage my store within the platform."

Color Schema

  • Primary Brand Color (`--color-brand-primary`): #007BFF - Used for major headings, buttons, and primary navigation elements.
  • Background Color (`--bg-secondary`): #F8F9FA - A light, neutral background color for sections and the main body.
  • Heading Text (`--text-heading`): #212529 - A dark color for optimal readability of headings.
  • Link Color (`--link-color`): #007BFF - The default color for hyperlinks and interactive text.

For implementing light and dark mode, a common approach is to use CSS variables and media queries. You can find more information on this technique [here](https://web.dev/prefers-color-scheme).

Typography

The following fonts will be used to maintain a consistent and readable design.

  • Headings: The 'Merriweather' serif font will be used for all headings (H1, H2, H3) to give the site a classic and authoritative feel.
  • Body Text: The 'Roboto' sans-serif font will be used for all paragraphs, lists, and general body text for its modern and clean readability.
