Our Story

iMarket was born from a vision to create a centralized, secure, and user-friendly e-commerce platform for the diverse and vibrant Kenyan market. We recognized the need for a single, trusted destination where customers could find everything from vehicles and real estate to electronics and office supplies, all from reputable local vendors.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower both customers and businesses by providing a seamless and reliable online shopping ecosystem. We strive to bring together diverse offerings under one brand, fostering economic growth and simplifying the digital shopping experience for everyone in Kenya.

Our Values

  • Trust: We build trust by ensuring every vendor on our platform is verified and every transaction is secure.
  • Innovation: We continuously innovate to improve our platform, making it more intuitive and accessible for our users.
  • Community: We are committed to supporting local businesses and building a strong community of vendors and loyal customers.
