About Soko Properties

Connecting you to your dream property in Kenya.

Our Story

Soko Properties was founded with a deep understanding of the Kenyan real estate market and a passion for connecting people with their ideal homes and investment opportunities. Since our inception in 2015, we have grown to become a trusted name, known for our personalized approach, extensive listings, and commitment to client satisfaction.

We pride ourselves on simplifying the property search and acquisition process, making it seamless and stress-free for both buyers and sellers. Our journey is defined by successful matches and happy clients who have found their perfect space through our dedicated efforts.

Our Mission

Our mission is to be the leading real estate platform in Kenya, empowering individuals and businesses to make informed property decisions. We achieve this by:

  • Providing a comprehensive and up-to-date database of diverse properties.
  • Offering transparent, reliable, and professional real estate services.
  • Utilizing innovative technology to enhance the property search experience.
  • Building lasting relationships based on trust, integrity, and mutual respect.

Our Values

Integrity

We uphold the highest ethical standards, ensuring honesty and transparency in all our dealings.

Innovation

We continuously seek and implement new technologies and strategies to improve our services and your experience.

Client-Centricity

Your needs are at the core of everything we do. We tailor our services to provide personalized solutions.

Reliability

We are a dependable partner, committed to delivering on our promises and providing accurate property information.
