About AutoGiant Motors

Driving excellence, one customer at a time.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, AutoGiant Motors began with a simple vision: to create a transparent and trustworthy environment for buying and selling vehicles in Kenya. What started as a small dealership has grown into a leading automotive hub, known for its extensive inventory, competitive pricing, and unparalleled customer service.

Over the years, we've built a reputation for integrity and dedication, helping thousands of Kenyans find their perfect ride. We believe that buying a car should be an exciting and straightforward experience, and we're committed to making that a reality for every client.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses in Kenya with reliable, high-quality vehicles that meet their diverse needs and aspirations. We strive to achieve this by:

  • Offering a diverse selection of new and pre-owned cars, SUVs, and motorcycles.
  • Ensuring transparent pricing and flexible financing options.
  • Providing expert advice and exceptional customer service from inquiry to after-sales support.
  • Fostering long-term relationships built on trust and mutual respect.

Our Values

Integrity

We conduct all our business with honesty, transparency, and ethical practices, building trust with every interaction.

Quality

We are committed to offering only the highest quality vehicles, thoroughly inspected and vetted for your peace of mind.

Customer Focus

Your satisfaction is our priority. We listen, understand, and tailor solutions to meet your unique automotive needs.

Innovation

We embrace new technologies and approaches to enhance the car buying experience and offer modern vehicle solutions.
